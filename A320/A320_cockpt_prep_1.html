NOTE: about A/SKID and Brakes

A/SKID prevents the wheels from blocking when braking, like the ABS in a car.

If failure on yellow AND green HYD systems, ACCU will be needed for braking (it will provide 7 manual brake applications, no autobrake)

For the use of ACCU braking, A/SKID must be deactivated.
